Features

Built to help you practice, track, and win. A quick glance at what TargetSmasher offers.

Smart Syllabus Tracking

Enter your subjects & class—TargetSmasher tracks your progress, distributes syllabus using ML, and suggests your daily bare-minimum study plan.

Duplicate Task Detection

Automatically detects repeated tasks, flags duplicates, and keeps your to-do list clean and meaningful.

Deadline Alerts & Notifications

Set deadlines and get reminders, last-hour alerts, and warnings when you’re close to missing a target.

AI Study Coach

A built-in chatbot helps you plan daily & weekly goals, break down big tasks, and stay motivated with smart suggestions.

Performance Analytics

Weekly & monthly reports showing strengths, weaknesses, consistency, and task completion patterns.

End-of-Day Micro Quiz

After completing a learning task, get a short quiz based on the chapter to reinforce what you learned.

Points & Rewards

Assign difficulty levels (Easy-100, Medium-200, Hard-400) and earn points to build streaks and unlock levels.

Daily Performance Comparison

Compare today's performance with yesterday’s—tasks completed, points earned, streaks, and focus hours.

Calendar-Based Review

Tap any date on the calendar to view previous targets, scores, task details, and what you achieved that day.

Daily Streak Coins

Maintain streaks to earn bonus coins every day—boosting motivation and long-term consistency.
